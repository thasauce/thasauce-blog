Via GAF:

_Microsoft held a &#8220;Xbox360 Revealed&#8221; launch party in Los
  
Angeles yesterday, and it appears that someone managed to get a picture
  
of the system and all the goods with a camera phone._
  
<img border="0" align="middle" src="http://sgtrama.no-ip.com/idear/everything.jpg" />

The console is standing up vertically on a stand. If you look closely,
  
you&#8217;ll notice what appears to be a hard drive attachment on top of it.

On the left side of the pic is the headset for Xbox Live, with the controller to the right of it.

Previously unseen is the camera, which is to the right of the controller. This should function like the EyeToy does with Sony&#8217;s PlayStation 2.

Now, there had already been partial pictures of the Xbox 360
  
revealed before, but there&#8217;s never been a picture showing the whole
  
console, along with the accessories, so this is a pretty big leak.
